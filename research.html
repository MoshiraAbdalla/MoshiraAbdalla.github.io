Research

Research Focus

My research focuses on scalable, interpretable, and language-aware video understanding systems.

Video Anomaly Detection

Weakly supervised, training-free, and scalable methods for identifying, localizing, and reasoning about abnormal events in complex real-world video scenes.

Vision-Language and Multimodal Learning

Leveraging large vision-language models to connect visual evidence with semantic and textual knowledge for improved anomaly recognition and interpretation.

Explainable AI for Video Understanding

Verbalized reasoning, interpretable predictions, and human-understandable anomaly descriptions that make video analysis systems more transparent.

Applied Computer Vision

Language-guided video analysis, NLP-based interactive systems, and graph-based medical image understanding for robust real-world deployment.
