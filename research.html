Research

Research Summary

My research focuses on developing weakly supervised, training-free, and scalable approaches for identifying, localizing, and reasoning about abnormal events in complex real-world video scenes.

Research Themes

Video Anomaly Detection

Weakly supervised, training-free, and scalable methods to identify, localize, and reason about abnormal events in surveillance videos.

Vision-Language & Multimodal Learning

Large vision-language models for aligning visual evidence with semantic and textual knowledge.

Explainable AI

Verbalized reasoning, interpretable predictions, and human-understandable anomaly descriptions.

Applied Computer Vision

Language-guided video analysis, NLP-based interactive systems, and graph-based medical image understanding.
